Are you Deployed?

If you would like to receive a care package from us, below is the information we will need. You can submit your request by email civiliansupplycorps@gmail.com
    Info we need from you:
  • Name and APO/FPO address
  • Expected date of return, so we know when to stop sending mail
  • Is the box just for you or to share in your unit? How many in unit?
  • Nickname for your unit (Nighthawks, etc)
  • Any special requests?
    Items we often have in stock:
  • Books
  • Hygiene – wipes, shampoo, qtips, toothbrush/paste/floss, soap
  • Quality of life – OTC medicine like Tylenol, acid reflux tabs, sheets, chapstick, vitamins
  • Snacks – cookies, crackers, jerky, drink mix, candy, poptarts
  • Note: Girl Scout cookies are donated to us in June, and are only available until we run out!
    Items we can get for you:
  • Anything that passes customs!
  • Extra clothes, socks – just provide size
  • Stationery to write home or journals
  • Shower shoes
  • Games, cards, videos, audio books
